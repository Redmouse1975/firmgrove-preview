Business plan

Mesa · real-time yield management for independent restaurants. Three-year plan, balanced to zero, wedge-first.

Executive summary

Mesa recovers revenue that expires nightly in independent restaurants. We win one dense neighbourhood at a time, seeding an opted-in diner pool that becomes a compounding local moat. Revenue is a low monthly base plus commission on filled tables. The plan below funds the Iberia wedge to a repeatable, fundable playbook.

Key assumptions

  • Diner pool is a proprietary asset that lowers CAC as each city matures
